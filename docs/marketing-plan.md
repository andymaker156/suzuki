# Blanc's Music Studios Marketing Plan

## Goal

Generate qualified inquiries from parents in Thornhill, Richmond Hill, Markham, North York, and the Greater Toronto Area who are looking for Suzuki piano lessons, Suzuki violin lessons, RCM preparation, flexible music lessons, or music lessons for very young children.

## Priority 1: Google Business Profile

This is the highest priority because parents searching on Google Maps or Google Search are already looking for lessons.

Steps:

1. Claim or create the Google Business Profile.
2. Use the real-world name consistently: `Blanc's Music Studios`.
3. Add complete contact details:
   - 21 Thorne Lane, Thornhill, Ontario L3T 5K6
   - 905-731-5336
   - jean_blanc@icloud.com
   - website URL once published
4. Choose categories:
   - Primary: Music school or Music instructor
   - Secondary: Piano instructor, Violin instructor
5. Add services:
   - Suzuki piano lessons
   - Suzuki violin lessons
   - Piano lessons for young children
   - Violin lessons for young children
   - RCM piano preparation
   - RCM violin preparation
   - Combined piano and violin lessons for families
   - Ukulele lessons for kids
   - Guitar lessons for kids
   - Customized music lessons
6. Add a short description:

Blanc's Music Studios is a family-run Suzuki violin and piano studio in Thornhill, Ontario. Jean (John) Blanc teaches Suzuki violin and Rose-Marie Blanc teaches Suzuki piano, offering a coordinated child-centred learning path for very young beginners, families, students preparing for Royal Conservatory of Music goals, and children who may benefit from flexible study options such as ukulele or guitar.

7. Add photos:
   - Teacher portraits
   - Piano teaching area
   - Violin teaching area
   - Studio entrance or exterior if appropriate
   - Music books, instruments, recital moments, and awards only if real and current
8. Post one update per month:
   - "Now accepting inquiries for Suzuki piano and violin lessons"
   - "How Suzuki supports very young beginners"
   - "Piano and violin lessons for siblings"
   - "Flexible music lessons for children interested in ukulele or guitar"

Success target:

- Profile verified.
- At least 10 strong photos.
- At least 10 parent/alumni reviews.
- All services listed.

## Priority 2: Reviews And Testimonials

Reviews are the strongest trust signal for parents comparing teachers.

Steps:

1. Create the Google review link or QR code from the Google Business Profile dashboard.
2. Ask long-term parents and alumni for honest reviews.
3. Do not offer discounts, gifts, or incentives for reviews.
4. Ask reviewers to write naturally about their real experience.
5. Reply to every review in a warm, professional tone.

Review request message:

Hello, we are updating the online profile for Blanc's Music Studios so local families can find Jean and Rose-Marie more easily. If your family had a positive experience with Suzuki piano, Suzuki violin, RCM preparation, customized lessons, BAPS, or lessons at Beverley Acres, would you be willing to leave an honest Google review? A few specific details about your child or family's experience would be very helpful.

Best review topics:

- Suzuki piano
- Suzuki violin
- Young beginners
- Child-centred teaching
- Parent involvement
- RCM preparation
- Flexible lessons, ukulele, or guitar exploration
- BAPS or Beverley Acres history
- Thornhill, Richmond Hill, Markham, or GTA

Success target:

- 10 reviews in the first 60 days.
- 25 reviews over 6 months.
- A few website testimonials with permission.

Steps:

1. Publish the homepage.

4. Add `Suzuki Piano Lessons in Thornhill`.

- Search terms appear naturally in headings and copy.

## Priority 4: Local Community Outreach

This should focus on parent communities rather than broad advertising.

Steps:

1. Reconnect with BAPS and Beverley Acres alumni networks if appropriate.
2. Ask alumni families to share the website or Google profile with younger families.
3. Share a small printed card or QR code with local parent networks.
4. Reach out to local schools, daycares, and community centers with a simple introduction.
5. Offer a short parent information session about Suzuki music learning for young children.

Best message:

Blanc's Music Studios offers Suzuki piano and violin lessons in Thornhill for very young beginners and families. Jean (John) Blanc and Rose-Marie Blanc bring decades of child-centred Suzuki teaching, Royal Conservatory affiliation, and a rare piano-plus-violin family studio environment.

Success target:

- 3 local referral partners.
- 1 parent information session.
- 5 inquiries from community referrals.

## Priority 5: Social Media

Social media should support trust, not replace Google search.

Recommended channels:

- Facebook Page: best for parents and local community sharing.
- Instagram: useful for credibility and short videos.
- YouTube Shorts: optional, useful for evergreen teaching tips.

Posting rhythm:

- 2 posts per month is enough if the content is real and high quality.

Content ideas:

- What makes Suzuki different for a 3-year-old beginner.
- Piano and violin under one family studio.
- Beethoven Society founder story: nearly 40 years of GTA music education, concerts, scholarships, and young artist support.
- How parents help with practice.
- RCM and Suzuki: how they can work together.
- Teacher story: Jean (John) Blanc.
- Teacher story: Rose-Marie Blanc.
- Studio photos and recital memories with permission.

Rules:

- Do not post identifiable children without written parent permission.
- Do not overuse generic stock images.
- Keep posts short and parent-focused.
- Always include a simple inquiry path: phone, email, or website.

Success target:

- Facebook Page created.
- Instagram profile created.
- 6 starter posts.
- Website link and phone/email visible on every profile.

## Priority 6: Light Paid Ads

Use paid ads only after Google Business Profile, reviews, website pages, and photos are in place.

Best first test:

- Google Search ad targeting "Suzuki piano Thornhill," "Suzuki violin Thornhill," "piano lessons Thornhill," and "violin lessons Richmond Hill."
- Small budget: $10-$20 per day for 2-4 weeks.
- Send traffic to the most relevant page, not just the homepage.

Avoid:

- Broad Facebook awareness ads.
- Generic "music lessons" ads across the entire GTA.
- Ads before reviews and real photos are ready.

Success target:

- Track calls and emails.
- Pause any keyword that brings irrelevant inquiries.
- Keep only ads that produce parent inquiries.
